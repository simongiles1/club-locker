# SOP: Cambridge Club House League Management

**Objective:** To execute the "Opt-Out" season transition, court reservation, and billing finalization within Club Locker.

### Phase 1: Season Creation & Initial Roster

1. **Navigate to Organized Play:** Select the **Box Leagues** tab.
2. **Clone the Season:** Find the most recent season (e.g., Spring 2026) and click the green **Create New Season** button.
3. **Configure the Pop-up:** * **New Season Name:** Follow the naming convention (e.g., "Cambridge Club Summer House League - [Start Date]").
* **Dates:** Set the Start and End dates for the new cycle.
* **Players per box:** Ensure this is set to **6**.
* **Move up/down:** Keep these at **0** as you will handle movements manually.


4. **Remove Opt-Outs:** Once the new season is created, go to the player list. Manually remove players who emailed early to opt out.
Use this process whenever a player emails to opt out before the season or pulls out due to injury:
4.1 Go to **Organized Play** -> **Box Leagues**.
4.2 Click the **Manage** button for the relevant league.
4.3 **The Two-Step Removal:**
    * **Step A:** Locate the player in their assigned Box. Either drag them into the **Registered Players** list on the left, or click the **"X"** next to their name in the box.
    * **Step B:** Once their name appears in the **Registered Players** list, click the **"X"** next to their name again to fully remove them from the league.
4.4 **Re-balancing:** If a removal leaves a box with 4 or fewer players, promote/demote players from adjacent boxes to keep the numbers balanced.
4.5 **Manual Seeding:** Review the boxes. Club Locker defaults to rating-based order, but you must manually move players up/down based on their recent performance or "inactive" ratings to ensure competitive parity.

### Phase 2: The Two-Week "Opt-Out" Window

1. **Initial Communication:** Send an email to the entire current list.
* **The Message:** State they are automatically enrolled and have **two weeks** to opt out.
* **The Schedule:** Include the static template (1v2, 3v4, 5v6, etc.) so they know their time slots based on their box position.


2. **Box Adjustments:** As more players opt out during this window, adjust the boxes to keep them at 6 players. If you have gaps, promote/demote players between boxes to balance the numbers.

### Phase 3: Court Management (Bulk to Individual)

1. **Bulk Booking (The "Hold"):** Two weeks before the start, use the Bulk Booking tool to highlight all league time slots. Save this as one giant reservation to "lock" the courts from general member bookings.
2. **Conversion (Thursday/Friday prior):** * **Cancel the Bulk Block:** Delete the large reservation to clear the slots.
* **Individual Entry:** Immediately book the 8 time slots per court (16 total per night).
* **Player Assignment:** Enter the specific names of the two opponents for each match.
* **Notification:** Once saved, Club Locker will automatically trigger confirmation emails to those specific players.



### Phase 4: Season Start & Billing Finalization

1. **Grace Period:** Allow the first 1–2 weeks of the season for last-minute injury withdrawals or late opt-outs.
2. **The "Two-Match" Rule:** * If a player drops out **before** playing 2 matches, they may be removed from the billing list.
* If a player has played **2 or more matches**, they are committed to the full season fee regardless of injury.


3. **Accounting Export:** * Finalize the entrant list.
* **Export to Excel:** Download the final roster.
* **Submit:** Email the CSV/Excel file to the accounting department for processing.



---

### Pro-Tip for the Employee:

> When a player drops out mid-season due to injury, prioritize filling that spot from the "waiting list" or moving a player from the box immediately below to keep the box at 5 or 6 players. This prevents the "BYE" week frustration for the remaining 4 players.
